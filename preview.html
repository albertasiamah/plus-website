Our Story

10th Capital Investments Limited was founded with a mission: to democratize wealth-building for Ghanaians. We recognized a critical gap in the financial market ‚Äî most investment products were inaccessible, complex, or required large minimum investments that kept everyday people out of the wealth-creation game.

We saw families saving their money in bank accounts earning single-digit interest rates, while their purchasing power eroded with inflation. We saw young professionals wanting to invest globally but facing technical and financial barriers. We saw talented entrepreneurs unable to access the capital markets. The problem was clear: investment access in Ghana was broken.

That's why we built Plus. We partnered with SEC-regulated institutions, integrated with trusted global platforms like Bamboo, and created an app that puts institutional-quality investment products directly into your hands. No middlemen. No hidden fees. No minimums that don't make sense. Just clear, transparent access to mutual funds, government securities, and international stocks ‚Äî all through one beautiful, easy-to-use app.

Today, over 100,000 Ghanaians trust Plus with their investment journey. We're just getting started.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Plus, our products, and how to get started.

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

+

Download the Plus app from Google Play or the App Store. Open the app and select "Sign Up". You'll need your email, phone number, and identification (national ID, passport, or driver's license). Complete the KYC verification process, and you're ready to start investing!

Is Plus available in my country?

+

Plus is currently available to Ghanaians. However, US stocks are available to users in multiple African countries. Check the app to see if your country is supported.

Is Plus safe? Are my investments protected?

+

Yes. Plus is regulated by Ghana's Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). All mutual funds are held with certified custodians. Your data is protected with bank-grade encryption. While we work hard to protect your information, investments in securities do carry market risk ‚Äî the value of your investments can go up or down.

Investing

What's the minimum investment amount?

+

Mutual Funds: Minimum of GHS 100 for initial investment, then GHS 50 for top-ups. Treasury Bills: Minimum of GHS 1,000. US Stocks: Start investing with as little as $1.

How do I fund my investment account?

+

You can fund your Plus wallet using MTN Mobile Money, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo Money, or bank transfer. Choose your preferred method in the app, enter the amount, and your wallet is funded instantly.

Can I withdraw my money anytime?

+

Mutual Funds: Yes, you can redeem anytime during market hours, with proceeds arriving within 2-3 business days. US Stocks: Yes, during US trading hours. Treasury Bills: Not until maturity. You can sell on the secondary market if needed.

Transactions

What are the fees?

+

Fees vary by product. Mutual funds typically charge a management fee. Treasury Bills may have a small origination fee. US stocks have per-transaction fees. All fees are shown clearly before you confirm your investment. There are no hidden charges.

How long does a transaction take?

+

Wallet top-ups are instant. Mutual fund purchases are processed within 48 business hours. Treasury Bill purchases settle immediately. US stock purchases settle within standard market hours (Mon-Fri, 9:30 AM - 4:00 PM EST).

Can I transfer money between my accounts?

+

Yes. You can transfer between your Plus wallet, mutual fund accounts, and US stock account. Internal transfers are instant. Withdrawals to your bank account or mobile money are processed within 24 hours.

Support

How do I contact customer support?

+

Contact our support team via email at support@plusapp.online. You can also reach out through the in-app chat support. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.

Still Have Questions?

Our support team is here to help. Email us or download the app to chat with us directly.
